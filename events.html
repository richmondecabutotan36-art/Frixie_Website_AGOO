Festivals & Events

Experience the vibrant culture, traditions, and celebrations of Agoo, La Union

Dinengdeng Festival

Month: May

This is the annual town fiesta of Agoo, celebrating the town’s culture, traditions, and devotion through street dancing and festivities.

Feast of Our Lady of Charity

Month: May

A religious celebration honoring the patron saint of Agoo, featuring masses, processions, and community gatherings.

Cultural Festivals

Month: Various

Events showcasing local traditions, dances, food, and crafts that reflect the identity of the Agoo community.
